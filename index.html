Why waste food when so many are hungry?

BestBeFed is a not-for-profit platform aimed at reducing food waste and helping people in need.

Feed yourself, and free yourself

Are you an individual struggling to pay your weekly grocery bill?

Our platform can match you to stores that are offering up their unsold - but still unexpired - products at a reduced price or even completely free. All you have to do is pick them up!

Make a difference!

Are you a grocery or convenience store owner who wants to give back to your community and spark positive change in the world?

Our AI-powered platform will use inventory and sales data to automatically notify you when food and other perishables are likely expire before sale. We will then connect you with food banks or individuals in need, so your unsold products can be used for good instead of ending up in landfills.
